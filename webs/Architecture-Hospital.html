Ruikang Hospital Fengling South Project

瑞康医院凤岭南项目

Several critical tasks were undertaken to ensure the scientific and practical design of the hospital. Road terrain modeling served as the project's foundation. Through precise earthwork calculations and cut-and-fill analysis the earthwork operations were optimized reducing costs and improving construction efficiency. Elevation analysis helped accurately plan the heights of buildings and roads avoiding construction challenges due to uneven terrain. Sunlight analysis ensured adequate lighting and ventilation for the hospital buildings enhancing patient comfort and recovery environment. Additionally, PSD layout work for the national-level award report made the project presentation materials more intuitive and visually appealing improving the overall image and presentation effect of the project.

负责进行多项关键任务，确保设计的科学性和实用性。首先，道路地形建模是项目的基础，通过精准的土方计算和填挖方分析，优化了土方工程，节省了成本并提高了施工效率。高程分析帮助准确规划建筑物和道路的高度，避免了地形高低不平带来的施工难题。日照分析则确保了医院建筑的采光和通风条件，提升了患者的舒适度和康复环境。此外，瑞康凤岭南医院部级报优的PSD排版工作，通过专业的设计排版，使得项目汇报材料更加直观和美观，提升了项目的整体形象和汇报效果。
