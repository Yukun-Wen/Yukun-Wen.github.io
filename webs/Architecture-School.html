Guilin City Lingui District Zhongyin Experimental Primary School Project

桂林市临桂区中隐实验小学项目

In this project, I undertook several key tasks to ensure the project's smooth progress and high-quality standards. First, I conducted site SketchUp modeling, providing an intuitive visual display through 3D modeling to help all parties better understand and evaluate the design plan. Next, I carried out a preliminary analysis of the plan, thoroughly researching project needs to ensure a scientific and feasible design scheme. In terms of text layout, I organized and formatted relevant text materials, making the information transmission clearer and more orderly. I also drew floor plans and section drawings for each building unit, providing detailed design references for each building unit. Specifically for teaching buildings 3#, 4#, 5#, 6#, and 7#, I completed the floor plans, section drawings, interior decoration tables, and energy-saving calculation tables, providing complete technical information for the preliminary design of these buildings. Additionally, I was responsible for the preliminary design drawings of the 1# canteen comprehensive building and the 2# teaching building, covering floor plans, section drawings, and interior decoration tables. Through these tasks, I not only demonstrated my professional skills and attention to detail but also ensured the comprehensiveness and accuracy of the project design. My work laid a solid foundation for the smooth implementation of the project, highlighting the core role and importance of design in construction projects. Through scientific design and meticulous work, I made a significant contribution to achieving a high-quality educational building project.
